Hoon School

Hoon is a strictly typed functional programming language that compiles itself to Nock (Urbit's low-level homoiconic combinator language) and is designed to support higher-order functional programming without requiring knowledge of category theory or other advanced mathematics.

Hoon School exists so you can have fun with others learning Hoon.

Students who finish a season's curriculum will receive the following:
-> A planet to develop on Urbit
-> A special and very limited piece of Urbit merch
